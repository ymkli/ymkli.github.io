Research

I work across atmospheric chemistry, climate impacts, and environmental data science, with an emphasis on problems where exposure, ecosystems, and emissions meet.

01

Wildfire smoke and ozone exposure

Quantifying how fire smoke reshapes ozone pollution and health burden, especially as wildfire regimes intensify under climate change.

02

Climate stressors and ecosystems

Studying how compound hot-dry events and large-scale atmospheric circulation affect vegetation productivity in drylands and northern ecosystems.

03

High-resolution emissions

Building and analyzing detailed emission inventories to understand mobility, greenhouse gases, and post-disruption rebound dynamics.
